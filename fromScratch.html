The Joys of Making Banana Bread

Why YOU Should Make Banana Bread:

  • It tastes super yummy
  • It helps you practice your baking on a low-stakes project
  • It makes people happy! For example:
    1. It makes YOU happy because you now know how to make banana bread!
    2. It makes your mom happy because now she no longer has to make you banana bread!
    3. It makes your friends happy because they get to partake of the banana bread!
